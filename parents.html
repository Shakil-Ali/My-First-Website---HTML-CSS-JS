Mum

My mum was born on the 22nd of August 1980, in Sylhet, Bangladesh. She came to this country at a very young age and started attending Alma primary school, the local primary school. This is also the same one me and my sister attended. After completing her primary education she started secondary school at Enfield County. During her time there she completed her GCSE's, and went on to study business at Southgate and Barnet College. Before completing her studies she went to Bangladesh and got married to my dad.

After my mum got married she moved back to England with my dad. She started working various jobs so that she could move out of my nans. At one point she was working at a factory, packing and sorting household items. During this period she worked at 2 different factories. Later on she got a job at McDonald's, working as a customer assistant. My mum had to stop working when I was born in 1999.

Dad

My dad was born on the 3rd of July 1977, and was raised in Sylhet, Bangladesh. He studied at a madrasa (a college for Islamic instruction), until the age of 16. Whilst studying he would help out with farm chores, as well as running his own stall in the local market. He primarily sold vegetables and snacks, but did also sell basic necessities. The vegetables he would sell were grown on our farm. When my dad was 20, he started helping teach the elder individuals in the village community. My dad got married to my mum on the 7th of July 1997, and moved to the UK.

After coming to England, my dad started working as a chef. He worked at a few restaurunts in his 10 years experience, and also owned his own takeaway. The takeaway was called Massala Queen. My dad sold his takeaway after a couple years and got his PCO license, and has now been working as a mini cab driver for the last 4 years.
